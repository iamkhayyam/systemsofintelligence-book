**[01:50] [Visual: Map showing Ostrom's research sites globally—some marked as failed, many as successful, highlighting the pattern.]**

**COMMONS BUILDER:**
"Failed commons had poor coordination. Successful commons had effective coordination between the resource, the users, and the governance system. The tragedy of the commons is a coordination failure, not an inevitability."
