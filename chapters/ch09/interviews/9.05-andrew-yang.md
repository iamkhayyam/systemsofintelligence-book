## Pre-Interview Context

**Who is Andrew Yang:**

- Entrepreneur and founder of Venture for America
- 2020 Democratic presidential candidate running on Universal Basic Income
- Founder of the Forward Party
- Author of "The War on Normal People" and "Forward"
- Champion of adapting economic distribution to technological displacement

**Why This Interview Matters:**
This interview demonstrates how automation isn't the problem—coordination failure between technological capability and economic distribution is. Yang's UBI proposal shows how coordinating technological abundance with human needs through new distribution mechanisms can transform automation from threat to opportunity.

**Key Themes:**

- Automation as coordination challenge
- Three bodies: technological displacement, human needs, distribution mechanism
- UBI as coordination solution
- Alaska Permanent Fund as proof of concept
- Two futures: coordinated vs. uncoordinated automation
